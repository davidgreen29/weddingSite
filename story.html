Our Story

The story of Ryan and I began in November 2020. I first noticed Ryan when she almost hit me with a floor cleaner at ALDI. I thought that girl is cute and I think I should try to talk to her. My only hope was her teeth would look normal under her mask. We met for our first date and immediately felt comfortable with each other and had a lot of common things of importance. Fast forward to March 2021, and we told each other that we love each other. December 2021 is when the ring talk started and Feb, 2022 is when it concluded with me asking Ryan to marry me. I am thankful God gave me such a caring woman with beautiful eyes, hair, and nice teeth lol. If you want to hear more details or some funny anecdotes talk to me or Ryan.
